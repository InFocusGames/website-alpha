Hello, connected world.

At Indizr, we're passionate about helping content creators reach audiences more directly online, without overly relying on Big Tech platforms as middlemen.

To accomplish that, we help organizations and individuals implement Web 3.0 technologies. That term is still evolving in the tech world, but in short, we'd say it encompasses anything that helps to decentralize the web.

That includes:

  • Blockchain
  • Semantic HTML
  • Containerization
  • Self-hosted and/or open-source content management
  • Linux

If you or your organization could benefit from pursuing a Web 3.0 strategy, let's talk.

Indizr's founder is Peter A. McKay, a Bay Area-based entrepreneur, consultant, and former prize-winning reporter for the Wall Street Journal and Washington Post. He's reachable directly anytime via email or Twitter.
