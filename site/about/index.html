News is too important to leave to Big Tech

Publishers and users have become way too reliant on massive tech companies' platforms to deliver the news. We're here to help fix that.

We believe the real solution isn't to optimize better for the big platforms, or to harangue them into adding better delivery features. It's to end the over-reliance. Just get news to people more directly.

That's no small task, to be sure. That's why we're interested in working with news organizations over time to:

  • Integrate new technologies.
  • Develop new ones as needed.
  • Manage projects.
  • Manage change more broadly.
  • Advise senior leadership.
  • Train staff.
  • Devise and track new metrics for independence.

If you need outside help with these areas of innovation, or others, let's talk more. Just drop us an email at admin@indizr.com to get the conversation going.
